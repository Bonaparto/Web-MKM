About series

The Maze Runner is a series of young adult dystopian science fiction novels written by American author James Dashner. The series consists of The Maze Runner (2009), The Scorch Trials (2010) and The Death Cure (2011), as well as two prequel novels, The Kill Order (2012) and The Fever Code (2016), a novella titled Crank Palace (2020), and a companion book titled The Maze Runner Files (2013). The sixth novel, titled The Maze Cutter, set 73 years following the events of The Death Cure, is set to be released on October 3, 2022.
The series, revealing details in non-chronological order, tells how the world was devastated by a series of massive solar flares and coronal mass ejections.

Series story

The action happens in post apocalypse world. Group of teenagers live in a safe zone of a giant maze, trying to get out of there for a long time. They can't get out because of dangers inside maze.
Finally, when they were able to get out, they found out it was part of a plan of some organisation and world is destroyed.
Then this group escapes from this organisation and go against it. They have confrontations, go through a lot of difficulties.
Series ends with organisation's collapse. Cure from virus, which destroyed world was invented. Group of people survived and started living on island.
